Broadcasting Software: OBS Studio

Uses Of OBS Studio

Open Broadcaster Studio or OBS is used to capture what users are seeing on the screen and either record it or upload it for the live viewers. You can also use OBS to record tutorials, gameplay videos and live streams to the internet.


Why did we choose this Broadcasting Software?

The Broadcasting Software that our group chose is OBS Studio, because it is more convenient for us since most of our members are not very familiar with other Broadcasting Softwares, and also one of our groupmate knows how the software runs. OBS Studio is convenient for our group because it can be easily learned by beginners.

Summary of Data

To summarize, the data shows the stream that was conducted by two groups (collab) gained more positive results than bad ones. In the data that was collected, we can see that there were more female viewers (10) than male viewers (6) . The stream that was conducted did reach the viewers’ expectations. The hosts were able to execute their roles correctly gaining the highest rate of 5 towards the stream. Even though the majority of the participants answered none of the questions regarding the improvements, there are some that suggest that the thing that we need to improve in our stream is that there should be more interactions with the viewers by entertaining them and talking more often in the stream. Overall, our stream is likely to be recommended to others.
